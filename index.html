Sanskrit Dating

A category-free relative chronology of Sanskrit texts, anchored to absolute dates.

Open the interactive chronology map →

Static overview — click to open the interactive, searchable version

The chronology is inferred from intertextual citation structure together with a stylometric clock (morphology + function-word n-grams), then resolved into absolute dates by a Gibbs sampler anchored on externally-dated works.

  • Interactive map — hover for dates & notes, search by title/author, zoom into any period
  • Full static rendering (all works)
  • Source, scripts & data on GitHub
Method and reproduction steps are documented in the repository README.
